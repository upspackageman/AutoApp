Cookie and Local Storage Policy

This Cookie and Local Storage Policy outlines how Auto-Complaints.com uses cookies and local storage technologies to enhance your experience on our website. It explains what cookies and local storage are, how we use them, and your choices regarding their use.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser when you visit a website. They enable the website to remember your actions and preferences (such as login information and language preferences) over time.

What is Local Storage?

Local storage is a web storage feature that allows web applications to store data persistently in the userâ€™s browser. It is similar to cookies but can hold larger amounts of data (up to 5MB or more depending on the browser). Data stored in local storage remains even after the browser is closed.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies on our website:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functionalities such as page navigation and access to secure areas of the website.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website, such as which pages are visited most often and if users receive error messages. This data helps us improve the website's performance.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies allow our website to remember choices you make (such as your username, language, or region) and provide enhanced, more personalized features.
  • Targeting/Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver advertisements that are relevant to you and your interests. They are also used to limit the number of times you see an advertisement and help measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.

How We Use Local Storage

We use local storage for the following purposes:

  • Storing User Preferences: We may store user preferences and settings to improve your experience on our website.
  • Session Management: Local storage may be used to manage user sessions and keep you logged in between visits.
  • Analytics and Performance: We may use local storage to collect analytics data to understand how users interact with our website and improve our services.

Your Choices

Cookies:

  • Consent: By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.
  • Opt-Out: You can choose to opt out of cookies by adjusting your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website.

Local Storage:

  • Control: You can control local storage through your browser settings. Most modern browsers allow you to clear local storage or block specific sites from using it.

Data Retention

We will retain cookie and local storage data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable laws.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information collected through cookies and local storage. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie and Local Storage Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on our website with an updated effective date.
